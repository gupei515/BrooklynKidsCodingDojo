Animate Your Name

In this project, we're going to learn how to animate your own name! Buckle up and lets learn some scratch!!!! The girl's name we are using at this example is "Olivia"

If your name don't have 7 characters, choose any of your favorite characters.

1) Color Clicker

2) Make spin

3) Play a sound

4) Dancing letter

5) Change size

6) Press a key

7) Glide around

8) Share your project with others, favor and comment on each others projects. (the purpose of this step is to show the social aspects of Scratch, and at same time prepare them to get enabled to become Scratcher so they can start using features like cloud variables)
