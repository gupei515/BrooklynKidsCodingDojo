Clicker Chase

In this project, we're going to make a game about fast clicking! Get your finger ready!

1) Pick out a backdrop and two character sprites. One will be the chaser and the other will be the runner.

2) Make a variable to tracking how many times you've clicked. Whenever you click on the stage, gain a click.

3) Make your chaser start on the left when the game starts. Make it slowly move to the right and touch your runner.

4) When your chaser touches your runner, play a sound and end the game.

5) Whenever you click, send the chaser back four or five

6) Use costumes to animate your runner and chasers while you click.

Bonus) Make your chaser slowly speed up over time (very slowly).
